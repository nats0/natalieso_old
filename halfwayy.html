Halfway There Van Frames

During fall 2021, the founders of Halway There Van Frames reached out to see if I would be interested in creating some renders for their products. They are a small start-up based in Los Angeles and are the Ikea of DIY van framing. They deliver the materials pre-cut to your door and provide simple instructions for users to easily transform elegantly designed van frames into their very own home-on-wheels.

Timeline:

Nov 2021 - Dec 2021

Deliverables:

  • Create three unique styles and finishes to show the versatility of their van frames
  • Produce three camera angles to match existing photography of the real van

Apps Used:

  • Blender
  • Adobe Photoshop
