Welcome to my 2D animation playground! This is a collection of shorts I've made on the side for fun. My workflow includes a combination of Adobe Illustrator, Premiere Pro and After Effects

Professor Aaron Siegel

Prompt:

  • Use rotoscoping, tracking, and compositing techniques to create a short (10 second) video of a fictional graphic user interface experience, either touchscreen/surface based or holographic (think Minority Report or Tron 2). Your interface should be an original design that you have created in Adobe Illustrator and After Effects, and the footage of the user interaction should be original footage you film yourself.

Prompt:

  • Create a 6 second animation that loops seamlessly. Your animation should be non-figurative and use only black, white, and a single hue.

Process:

  • I've been ethalled by Rube Goldberg machines ever since I was a kid, especially when it's a complex contraption designed to complete a simple task. Combining the need for the animation to loop seamlessly and my love of basketball, I created the Rube Goldberg-inspired: "Bucket Machine".
